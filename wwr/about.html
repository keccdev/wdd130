About Us?

At La Ceiba Whitewater Rafting our passion is to bring excitement and adventure to the hearts of nature and adrenaline lovers. Founded in 1996, we are proud to be leaders in the rafting industry and provide unforgettable experiences on the most exciting rivers. Our mission is simple: we want you to experience nature in a unique, exciting and safe way.

History

The Cangrejal River, which meanders between the Pico Bonito and Nombre de Dios national parks, became the perfect setting for this new activity. Its Class II, III, and IV rapids challenged intrepid boaters, and the lush jungle that surrounded it added a magical touch to the experience.

As more people joined this exciting journey, tour operators began offering rafting services, and professional guides were trained to ensure the safety of adventurers. The river became a call to those seeking more than the ordinary—an invitation to embrace the untamed, ride the wild currents, and discover the heart-pounding thrill of rafting.
