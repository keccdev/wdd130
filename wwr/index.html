Excursion to Cayos Cochinos

Do you want to enter an authentic paradise in the middle of the Caribbean Sea? With this excursion to Cayos Cochinos you will have the opportunity to see dreamlike places. What are you waiting for?

Looking for an extreme adventure?Looking The Pico Bonito National Park offers it to you. Between canopy, horseback riding, cycling, mountain camping, hiking, river snorkeling and a splendid view of the natural wealth, you will not even know where to start.

Cuero y Salado is a wonder, a place that encourages us to believe in and take care of our natural resources and know how to promote them. The people, its flora and fauna invite us to enjoy it.
